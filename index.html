Candidate

This is a candidate that is running to be nominated as President of the United States.

Joe Biden

Former Delaware Senator, Joe Biden, was elected as the 47th U.S. vice president with his running mate, President Barack Obama, in 2008 and re-elected in 2012. Joe Biden briefly worked as an attorney before turning to politics. He became the fifth-youngest U.S. senator in history as well as Delaware's longest-serving senator. He is now the 2020 Democratic presidential nominee.
